Tournament of Magicians


Project is finished, but no longer supported.

CODENAME: #card-game

TITLE: Tournament of Magicians

YEAR: 2016, 2017

SOFTWARE: Unity(C#), Gimp, Audacity, Notepad++

Huge and ambitious project started in October 2016 and finished a year later. Major improvement over my previous projects. My first game written completely in C# instead of UnityScript. Some of the graphics were created by me (including all the portraits) and I did the whole programming.
-Fully working game - 40 different cards and 9 characters.
-Working demo saving and loading system - possibility of watching match again.
-GUI Scalling which allows to play in 4:3 (like 1024x768) or in 16:9 (ex.1920x1080) with some additional visible cards.
-Project had also working multiplayer, but to be honest it had many bugs.
Funfact: Most of the game (excluding menu) runs on single C# script which contains almost 3000 lines of code.
