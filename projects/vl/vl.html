VillageLord


One of my current projects.

CODENAME: #Project_Click

TITLE: VillageLord

YEAR: 2018

SOFTWARE: Unity(C#), Gimp, Audacity, Notepad++

Project for "The Fall All-OGA Game Jam! 2018". Idea was to create idle game, but I abandoned that concept and turned it into strategy/economic game. There are 3 major systems in this game: Construction system - Click on a tile and put a building. Economy system - buildings generate one type of resource and require other, if you can't afford it - it won't generate anything. Military - recruit soldiers to defend your village. Game is also full of easter eggs and references.
