RPG2D


Project is abondoned and some files are lost.

CODENAME: #rpg2d

TITLE: unnamed

YEAR: 2015, 2016

SOFTWARE: Unity(JS), Gimp, Audacity, Notepad++

Project started on 9th September 2015 and continued to February. My first try with RPG games. I wanted to get something similar to games you can make in RPGMaker. Fight screen in this game was based on Disciples franchise by Strategy First. Whole project was made mainly for fun rather than something more serious. At least I've improved my skills a bit. I made scripts for movement, fighting, picking and equiping stuff. Graphics were also mine (well I shouldn't be proud of them, should I?).
